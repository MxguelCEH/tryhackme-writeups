# Nmap Room (TryHackMe)

## Objective
Learn how to scan and identify open ports using Nmap.

## Tools Used
- Nmap

## Commands
nmap -sS 10.10.10.10
nmap -sV 10.10.10.10

## Results
- Port 22 (SSH) is open
- Port 80 (HTTP) is open

## What I Learned
- How to perform port scanning
- Difference between scan types

## Key Concepts
- Open ports
- Services
- TCP scanning

## Conclusion
This lab helped me understand how attackers and defenders identify open services.
